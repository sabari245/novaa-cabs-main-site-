Career Opportunity

Become a professional chauffeur with RentQ and enjoy competitive earnings, flexible schedules, and the support of an industry leader.

About the Position

As a RentQ Professional Chauffeur, you'll be responsible for providing premium transportation services to our diverse clientele. This role requires exceptional customer service skills, safe driving practices, and a professional appearance.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide safe and timely transportation to clients
  • Maintain vehicle cleanliness and presentation
  • Offer exceptional customer service at all times
  • Adhere to all traffic laws and company policies
  • Handle client luggage and assist with special needs

Requirements

  • Valid driver's license with at least 3 years of driving experience
  • Clean driving record with no major violations
  • Successful completion of background check
  • Professional appearance and demeanor
  • Excellent communication and customer service skills
  • Minimum age of 25 years

Begin accepting rides and earning with RentQ's premium clientele.
